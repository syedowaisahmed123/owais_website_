PDA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

PDA (poojya Dodappa Appa) College of Engineering is located in the District of Gulbarga,which is Affilated to the VTU
The college is established in the year 1958 by HKE Society.
This society,founded by Shri Mahadevappa Rampure,a great Educationist.

This college is spread over a 70 acre campus on each side of the Mumbai-Chennai line P.D.A College is Affilited to the Visvesvaraya Technological university,belgaum.
PDA is one among the 14 college selected under TEQIP scheme,sponsered by world bank.
the college offeres Undergraduate courses in Electronic Engineering,civil engineering,computer science and engineering,mechanical engineering etc.
